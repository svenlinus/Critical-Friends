Our Approach

Critical Friends LLC is a consulting and coaching firm supporting leaders to execute strategies that improve diversity, equity, and inclusion within their organizations. Our team guides organizations how to implement practices that improve decision making through the lens of equity. Our unique perspectives and objective critiquing results in partnerships that produce sustainable, measurable growth and outcomes.

Vision

We envision high functioning communities where systems and institutions are restructured, equitable and produce positive outcomes for all despite race, culture, gender or ethnicity

Mission Statement

As Critical Friends, we support leaders to develop and execute transformative strategies that create equity by recognizing and eliminating unconscious bias within their organizations.

The Why

Leaders are actively engaged in doing equity work, contracting with organizations such as Race Works, Pacific Equity Group and Facing History Facing Ourselves. While this is critical work and needs to continue, principals also need ongoing support moving from theory to action. After workshops, principals are responsible for carrying the throughline with their staff. This requires thoughtful planning, debriefing, assessing data and an accountability partner over time. Principals/Leaders are faced with multiple competing priorities making their job nearly impossible.
